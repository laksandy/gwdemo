Objectives

Fast Rope Labs will assist GW MFA Ophthalmology department analyze patient data, including de-identified home address zip code, age, and sub-speciality, to develop a better understanding of patient catchment area and geographic profile. Such and understanding will facilitate follow-on analysis of patient demographic profile and competitive positioning to enable objective evaluation of target expansion locations.


Approach

  1. Data Import and Preparation: Gather data from GW MFA, import data into statistical analysis environment, and prepare data for analysis.
  2. Data Multi-Factor Analysis: Segment and separate data by age, sub-speciality, and location relative to the primary location.
  3. Data Multi-Factor Analysis: Segment and separate data by age, sub-speciality, and location relative to the primary location.
  4. Data Visualization and Reporting: Visualize data in map and graphical form to display catchment area by age, sub-speciality, and in aggregate for the 50th and 80th percentiles at the primary location. Compile report and deliver digitally to GW MFA.
  5. Competitor and Demographic Assessment: Identify competitive practices in and near the catchment areas, as well as demographic profile within the existing catchment area using Census, BLS and Medicare Data.
  6. Opportunity Assessment: Evaluate potential locations based on risk modeling from analyzed data
